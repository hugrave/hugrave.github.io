Twitter Analysis for May 31, 2019

Each indicator on the circumference of the biggest circle corresponds to a particular word. The circumference is divided into various sectors representing various communities and alognside is the word most used in that community.

There are multiple smaller circles corresponding to each sector and having the same color. These are the influencers for that particular community. The size of the circles showcase how many followers a community has.

Finally the lines represent what word is spoken by whom.

Hovering over a word indicator will display the word it represents. Hovering over an influencer circle will show a list of top influencers with their number of followers and will also highlight the words tweeted by those influencers.
